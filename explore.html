How It Began

enVision was founded in 2015 in Florence, Italy by Ebina.

She was a crafter and she wear glasses daily.

Everyday in front of the mirror she looked upset because the basic glasses always destroyed the outfits that she wore.

she had a thought to make glasses that are modern and fashionable, that can suit the outfits she wore, she thought that great glasses can boost confidence.
